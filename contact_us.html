About Us

Patriot® Exhaust Products is a member of the PerTronix Performance family. PerTronix has been in the aftermarket since 1962 and is an industry leader in electronic ignitions. The Patriot® Exhaust brand traces its roots back to the mid sixties as Thunderbird Products, a leading producer of exhaust products for Street Rod, Muscle Car and Air Cooled VW Markets. The Doug's Headers brand joined the family in 2005 and dates back even further, to 1958. So as you can see we are not new to the Performance Exhaust business - we bring 50+ years of performance design and manufacturing experience to our innovative motorcycle pipes and fuel injection tuners! Visit the PerTronix website at www.pertronix.com
